9/11 in Realtime

  • About 9/11 in Realtime

    9/11 in Realtime is a multimedia experiment for teachers, with the purpose of helping their students truly understand and absorb the events of September 11, 2001. We've collected media--video, audio and other items--available from the days before and after the September 11 attacks, syncrhonized them together and built a tool to help students be immersed in the events of the day.

    THE FOLLOWING WEBSITE MAY CONTAIN VISUALS, AUDIO AND OTHER CONTENT THAT SOME VIEWERS MAY FIND EXTREMELY DISTURBING.

    THIS TOOL IS INTENDED TO BE USED ALONGSIDE A CAREFULLY RESEARCHED AND APPROPRIATE CURRICULUM.

    For questions regarding this project, please email Robbie Byrd.
